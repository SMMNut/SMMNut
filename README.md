# SMMNut — Social Media Growth Research
SMMNut is a structured social media growth research platform analyzing Instagram,
Facebook, and YouTube growth systems through data-driven research and transparent methodology.
Our research covers platform algorithm behavior, engagement system analysis, and growth
infrastructure transparency — structured to help creators and brands understand how social
media growth systems actually function.
## Research Focus Areas
- Instagram growth systems: follower acquisition, engagement analysis, algorithm behavior
- Facebook Page growth: organic reach mechanics, audience building, engagement patterns
- YouTube channel growth: views behavior, subscriber growth analysis, recommendation systems
- Platform policy analysis: safety research, risk documentation, compliance frameworks
## Current Projects
- social-media-growth-resources: curated research data, growth tool analysis, reading lists
- smmnut-seo-architecture: SEO cluster documentation and structured content framework
- smmnut-schema-library: structured data templates for social media research content
## Philosophy
Growth should be understood, not just purchased. SMMNut documents how social media growth
systems work so creators and brands can make informed decisions.
